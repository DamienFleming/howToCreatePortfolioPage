How to start your portfolio page

Follow the steps below and you'll be well on your way!

STEP 1

If you haven't already, Visit Start Bootstrap to find a free theme and download it.

STEP 2

Create a new repository on github and add all of the files from the download.

STEP 3

If you'd like the page to be live on your main github page, make sure your repository name is [your-github-username].github.io

STEP 4

Modify your code: replace template information with your own, customize layouts and styles further to your liking, and develop your page as you see fit.
